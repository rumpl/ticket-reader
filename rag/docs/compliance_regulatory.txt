# Acme Corp — Compliance & Regulatory Framework

## Regulatory Landscape (COMP-001)
**Last Updated:** January 2025

Acme Corp operates in the B2B SaaS space serving customers in the US and EU.
The following regulations apply to our operations:

### GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **Applies to**: All EU customer data and EU-based employees
- **Data Protection Officer**: Sarah Chen (dpo@acmecorp.com)
- **Lawful bases**: Contract performance (primary), legitimate interest, consent
- **Data Subject Rights**: Must respond within 30 days
  - Right of access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection
  - Automated process in BambooHR for employee requests
  - Customer requests routed via support → legal → engineering
- **Breach notification**: 72 hours to supervisory authority, "without undue delay" to affected individuals
- **Data transfers**: EU → US via EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF), SCCs as fallback

### SOC 2 Type II
- **Audit firm**: Deloitte
- **Audit period**: January – December (annual)
- **Trust Service Criteria**: Security, Availability, Confidentiality
- **Last audit**: Clean report, December 2024
- **Next audit**: December 2025
- **Key controls**:
  - Access reviews quarterly
  - Change management via PR process (see ENG-STD-001)
  - Incident response tested semi-annually
  - Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)

### CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **Applies to**: California-resident customer contacts
- **"Do Not Sell"**: Acme does not sell personal information
- **Consumer requests**: Same process as GDPR, 45-day response window

### PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry)
- **Scope**: Limited — we use Stripe as payment processor
- **SAQ-A**: Self-Assessment Questionnaire A (card data never touches our systems)
- **Annual attestation**: Completed via Stripe's compliance dashboard
- **Key requirement**: No storage, processing, or transmission of cardholder data

## Data Retention Policy (COMP-002)

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Method | Legal Basis |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Customer account data | Duration of contract + 2 years | Automated purge | Contract |
| Transaction records | 7 years | Automated purge | Tax/legal obligation |
| Application logs | 90 days (OpenSearch) + 1 year (S3) | Lifecycle policy | Legitimate interest |
| Audit logs | 3 years | Manual review + purge | SOC 2 / legal |
| Employee PII | Duration of employment + 6 years | Manual purge by HR | Legal obligation |
| Marketing consent | Until withdrawal + 30 days | Automated | Consent |
| Support tickets | 3 years after resolution | Automated purge | Legitimate interest |
| Backups | 30 days (database), 90 days (full) | Automatic rotation | Business continuity |

## Audit & Evidence Collection (COMP-003)

### Continuous Compliance
We maintain audit readiness through:
- **Automated evidence collection**: Vanta platform pulls evidence from AWS, GitHub, BambooHR
- **Quarterly access reviews**: All system access reviewed by managers via Vanta
- **Monthly vulnerability scans**: Snyk (dependencies) + AWS Inspector (infrastructure)
- **Semi-annual penetration tests**: Conducted by NCC Group
- **Annual tabletop exercises**: Incident response and disaster recovery simulations
