# Acme Corp — HR Policies & Employee Handbook

## Time Off Policy (HR-POL-001)
**Effective Date:** January 2025

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- **0-2 years tenure**: 22 days/year
- **2-5 years tenure**: 27 days/year
- **5+ years tenure**: 32 days/year
- PTO does not roll over beyond 5 days into the next year
- Requests via BambooHR, approved by direct manager
- Minimum 2 weeks notice for vacations >3 consecutive days

### Sick Leave
- 10 days/year, separate from PTO
- No documentation required for ≤3 consecutive days
- Medical certificate required for >3 consecutive days
- Unused sick days do not roll over or pay out

### Parental Leave
- **Primary caregiver**: 16 weeks fully paid
- **Secondary caregiver**: 6 weeks fully paid
- Can be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption
- Gradual return option: 80% schedule at full pay for 4 weeks after return

## Remote Work Policy (HR-POL-002)

Acme Corp operates a **hybrid-first** model:
- Minimum **2 days per week in office** (Tuesday and Thursday are anchor days)
- Fully remote positions available for approved roles (Engineering, Design, Data)
- International remote work allowed up to 30 days/year (tax compliance requirement)
- Home office stipend: $1,500 one-time setup + $75/month for internet

### Equipment
All employees receive:
- MacBook Pro (M4, 36GB RAM) or ThinkPad X1 Carbon (employee choice)
- 27" external monitor
- Keyboard, mouse, headset
- Equipment must be returned within 14 days of last working day

## Compensation & Benefits (HR-POL-003)

### Salary Reviews
- Annual review cycle in March
- Based on performance rating, market data, and internal equity
- Merit increase budget: typically 4-6% of payroll

### Benefits
- **Health insurance**: 100% employee premium covered, 80% for dependents
- **Dental & Vision**: Included in health plan
- **401(k)**: 4% company match, immediate vesting
- **Life insurance**: 2x annual salary
- **Professional development**: $3,000/year per employee
- **Wellness stipend**: $100/month (gym, mental health apps, etc.)

### Equity
- All full-time employees receive stock options at hire
- 4-year vesting schedule with 1-year cliff
- Refresh grants based on performance (annual review)

## Performance Management (HR-POL-004)

### Review Cycle
- **Quarterly check-ins**: Lightweight goal progress review with manager
- **Annual review** (March): Comprehensive evaluation with calibration
- **Rating scale**: Exceeds / Meets / Developing / Below Expectations

### Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
- Triggered by "Below Expectations" rating or manager discretion
- Duration: 30-60 days with clear, measurable goals
- Weekly check-ins with manager and HR
- Successful completion returns employee to regular standing
- Unsuccessful completion may result in termination

## Onboarding Process (HR-POL-005)

### Week 1
- Day 1: Welcome session, equipment setup, security training
- Day 2: Product overview and company strategy presentation
- Day 3-5: Team introductions, codebase walkthrough (engineering), tool access

### First 30 Days
- Assigned a buddy from the same team
- Complete all compliance training (security, anti-harassment, data privacy)
- Ship first PR or deliverable (engineering roles)
- 30-day check-in with manager

### First 90 Days
- Complete onboarding checklist in BambooHR
- Present a "What I Learned" at team meeting
- 90-day performance check-in with manager
- Probation period ends (confirmation of employment)
