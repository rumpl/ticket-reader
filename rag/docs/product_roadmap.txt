# Acme Corp — Product Roadmap & Strategy

## Company Overview
**Founded:** 2019 | **Employees:** 287 | **ARR:** $42M (Q1 2025)
**Mission:** Simplify supply chain visibility for mid-market manufacturers.

## Product Suite

### SupplyView (Core Product)
Real-time supply chain visibility platform.
- **Launched:** 2020
- **Customers:** 340+ mid-market manufacturers
- **Key features**: Real-time inventory tracking, supplier risk scoring,
  demand forecasting, automated purchase order generation
- **Tech stack**: Go backend, React frontend, PostgreSQL, Kafka, deployed on EKS

### SupplyView Analytics (Launched Q3 2024)
Advanced analytics and reporting add-on.
- **Adoption**: 45% of existing customers
- **Features**: Custom dashboards, predictive analytics, anomaly detection,
  exportable reports (PDF, CSV, API)
- **Tech stack**: Python (ML models), Go (API layer), ClickHouse (analytics DB)

### SupplyView Connect (Beta, Q1 2025)
Supplier collaboration portal.
- **Status**: Private beta with 20 customers
- **Features**: Supplier onboarding, document exchange, compliance tracking,
  real-time messaging between buyers and suppliers
- **GA target**: Q3 2025
